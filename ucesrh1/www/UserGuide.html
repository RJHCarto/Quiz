UCL Quizlet User Guide

Welcome to the quiz, where the aim of the game is to answer questions correctly and enjoy your scenic walk around University College London, while learning about the area.

live_help

The button above is used to display the quiz questions that have been set for you. Be sure to click the icon to get the questions.

Be sure to wait for the app to get your location, you must have confirmed this on your device in the popup on opening the app.

my_location

The next button above must be clicked to find which questions your are close to, the range being 20 metres. Markers on the map will go green if in range and available to answer.

Once clicked, the question in range will appear in a pop up, choose your answer carefully. Click submit once you have chosen from the list and you will find out if you are correct or incorrect. Your answer is now uploaded to our database.

Finally, continue your quizlet walk, reload the questions and find out if you are in range of any new questions! Good Luck!
